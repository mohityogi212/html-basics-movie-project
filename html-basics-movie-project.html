Top Movies to Watch!

These are some of my personal favorite movies that inspire, move, and entertain me deeply.

My Personal Favorites:

Baghban

A touching family drama about love, respect, and aging parents. Timeless and emotional.

Meiyazhagan

A heartfelt story showing pure friendship and human connection. Simple yet powerful.

The Lunchbox & Piku (Irfan Khan)

Beautifully subtle movies with soulful performances. They celebrate ordinary life with depth.

The Wolf of Wall Street

A high-energy tale of ambition and greed, filled with lessons on excess and discipline.

The Pursuit of Happyness

One of the most inspiring films about perseverance, hope, and never giving up.

Summary

Each of these films teaches something unique — about life, love, or ambition — making them must-watch classics.
